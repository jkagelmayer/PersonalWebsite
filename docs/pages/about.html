About Me

Hi! I'm a senior at Purdue University pursuing Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science, Aerospace Engineering, and Philosophy. I'll be graduating in May of 2020 and I'm looking for full-time work. I've also served as President of the Purdue Chapter of Sigma Gamma Tau and I work as a Supplemental Instruction leader for Aero courses.


What's left of my time is usually spent on some mixture of Dungeons & Dragons, video games, writing, and baking. I also listen to podcasts pretty much constantly.


When it comes to my career, I have a lot of different passions and skills. I love programming, but I also love being an engineer and solving problems. I also like to be creative and to talk to people. My hope is to find a job that lets me utilize all of those skills and really be my whole self - all while solving big, exciting problems.
