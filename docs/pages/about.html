About Me

Hi! I'm a senior at Purdue University pursuing a triple-major in Aerospace Engineering, Computer Science, and Philosophy. I've served as President of the Purdue Chapter of Sigma Gamma Tau and I also work as a Supplemental Instruction leader for Aero courses.


What's left of my time is usually spent on some mixture of Dungeons & Dragons, video games, writing, and baking. I also listen to podcasts pretty much constantly.


My future career path will heavily depend on that of my fiancé, Bee, but my hope is to work on software development and testing within the aerospace industry. I'm particularly interested in trajectory simulation, control systems, and machine learning.
